Privacy Policy

At OllyVerse, your privacy isn't an afterthought — it's a design principle. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

Last updated: February 2026 v1.0
Quick summary: We collect only what we need, we don't sell your data, and we treat every user with respect. Read the full policy below or contact us with any questions.

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you interact with us. This may include:

  • Contact details — your name and email address when you submit our contact form or reach out directly.
  • Communication content — messages, inquiries, or project briefs you send to us.
  • Technical data — browser type, IP address, and pages visited, collected automatically through standard web analytics.
  • Employment information — CVs or application details if you apply for a position via our Careers page.

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as payment information, government IDs, or biometric data.

Any information collected is used solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide the services you requested.
  • Improve our website experience and understand how users navigate our content.
  • Process and evaluate job applications submitted through our Careers page.
  • Send operational updates relevant to your interaction with us (no spam, ever).

We will never use your information for automated profiling or unsolicited marketing without your explicit consent.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Full stop.

We may share data with trusted third-party services strictly for operational purposes — such as web hosting, email delivery, or analytics. These providers are bound by their own privacy standards and are not permitted to use your data for any purpose beyond the service they provide to us.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of OllyVerse and its users.

Our website may use minimal cookies to support core site functionality and understand aggregate traffic patterns. We do not use invasive tracking, cross-site tracking scripts, or third-party advertising cookies.

You may configure your browser to reject or delete cookies at any time. Doing so will not prevent you from using our site, though some features may behave differently.

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. While we strive to protect your data using industry-standard practices, we cannot guarantee absolute security. We encourage users to exercise care when sharing personal information online.

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose it was collected for, or as required by applicable law.

Contact form submissions are typically retained for up to 12 months. Job applications are held for the duration of the recruitment process and deleted thereafter unless you give consent for us to retain them longer.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at officialOllyVerse@outlook.com.

You have the right to:

  • Correction — request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format where applicable.
  • Objection — object to certain types of processing, including direct marketing.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our services evolve or legal requirements change. When we do, we'll revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acknowledgement of the updated policy.

Have questions about your privacy?

We're happy to clarify anything in this policy or address any concerns you may have.
